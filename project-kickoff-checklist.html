Team Briefing
Get your team aligned before execution starts. Confusion now = revisions later.
Internal Setup
Project card created on tracking board
Add to the board with: client name, status = Backlog, deadline, assigned team members.
AM
Team members assigned to project
Confirm who is the designer, content creator, media buyer for this project.
AM
Written project brief shared with team
Use the Brief Template in Drive. Includes: background, audience, goals, tone, do's & don'ts.
AM
Team has read brief and raised questions
24-hour window for questions. After that, execution begins — no mid-project brief questions.
Team
Internal team WhatsApp group updated
Pin the brief doc link and deadline in the team group.
AM
Deliverable Scope Confirmed
Full deliverable list documented
Exactly what are we making? How many posts, ads, designs, reports? Count everything.
AM
Internal deadlines set (2 days before client deadlines)
Every deliverable has an internal due date. No exceptions.
AM
Revision rounds clarified with team (max 2 rounds)
Team knows what's included. Round 3 requires AM approval and may be charged.
Team

Setup & Access
Never chase for passwords mid-campaign. Get everything upfront.
Communication Channels
Client WhatsApp group created
Name: [ClientName] x [AgencyName]. Add: client PIC + Account Manager only.
AM
Communication preferences confirmed with client
WhatsApp, email, or calls? Response time expectations set on both sides.

TikTok / LinkedIn / other platforms access (if applicable)

AM
Google Ads / Meta Ads account access (paid campaigns)
Request via account sharing — never ask for personal login. Verify billing access if managing spend.
Media
Google Analytics / Search Console access
View access minimum. Editor access if making tag or goal changes.

Strategy & Planning
Plan the work before working the plan. Client alignment here saves revisions later.
Content & Campaign Strategy
Content calendar drafted for the month
Dates, platforms, content types, captions, and hashtags. Submitted to AM for review.
Creator
Content calendar approved by client (in writing)
Send as PDF or shareable Google Sheet. Get "YES" reply on WhatsApp before any design starts.
AM
Ad campaign brief prepared (if paid media in scope)
Objective, audience, budget split, creative requirements, landing page URL, tracking pixel.
Media
KPIs and success metrics agreed with client
What numbers are we chasing? Leads, reach, ROAS, clicks? Confirmed before launch.
AM
Production Scheduling
Graphic design production schedule set
Designer knows exactly what to produce and by when. No ambiguity.
Designer
Copywriting deadlines set
Copy must be approved before design starts. Schedule accounts for this dependency.
Creator
Photo or video shoot scheduled (if original content needed)
Location, date, call time, equipment list, talent confirmed.
Team
Strategy notes

Approval & Launch
Final checks before anything goes live. This is where quality is protected.
Pre-Launch Quality Check
All deliverables passed internal review
AM has reviewed everything. Spelling, branding, pricing, links all checked.
AM
Client has given written approval to publish
WhatsApp "YES" or email confirmation. Screenshot saved to Drive > Approvals folder.
AM
Image/video specs verified for each platform
Instagram Feed 1080x1080, Story 1080x1920, Facebook 1200x628, TikTok 1080x1920.
Designer
All links and CTAs tested
Landing page live, form works, tracking pixel firing, UTM parameters in place.
Media
Ad campaigns reviewed before going live
Targeting, budget, schedule, creative, pixel — double-checked by media buyer.
Media
Post-Launch
Client notified that work is live
Send screenshot or link confirming posts are published / ads are running.
AM
Project card updated to "In Progress" / "Completed" on board
Keep the tracking board current. This is the team's single source of truth.
AM
First 48-hour performance check scheduled (paid campaigns)
Set a calendar reminder. Flag to AM if CPM, CTR, or spend looks off.
Media
Monthly report reminder set for the 28th
Team submits performance notes by 28th. AM compiles and sends report by 5th of next month.
Team
